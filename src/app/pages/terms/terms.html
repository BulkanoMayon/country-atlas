Project Policy

Terms and Conditions

These terms explain the intended use of the CityExplore / Country Atlas application and its browser-based features.

1. General Use

This application is intended for educational, informational, and project demonstration purposes. Users may browse travel-related content and explore destinations through the provided interface.

2. Data Sources

Country and destination data are provided through external APIs and front-end fallback content. Accuracy and availability may vary depending on the source.

3. User Accounts

Registration and login are simulated using browser storage. User data is stored only on the current device and browser and is not saved in an online backend system.

4. Favorites and Saved Data

Favorites and user-related data are stored locally. Clearing browser storage or cache may remove saved information from the application.

5. External Map Links

Some pages provide access to external map services. The application is not responsible for the content, behavior, or availability of third-party sites.

6. Future Improvements

Features, design elements, and content may be updated as the project evolves. Additional lifestyle, city, tourism, and service-related content may be included later.
